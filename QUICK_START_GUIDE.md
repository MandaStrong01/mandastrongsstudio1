### Step 1: Sign In to MandaStrong App
Use one of these authorized emails:

### Step 2: Connect Your Google Account
After signing into MandaStrong:
- Go to any upload page
- Click "Connect Google Drive"
- Sign in with YOUR Google account
- Grant permissions

## What You Can Do

Once both steps are complete, you can:

- Import files from YOUR Google Photos
- Import files from YOUR Google Drive
- Upload files from URLs
- Upload local files
- View ALL team members' uploaded files
- Manage shared projects
- Collaborate on movie projects

## Privacy & Sharing

**Private to You:**
- Your MandaStrong login
- Your Google account connection
- Which Google files you can browse

**Shared with Team:**
- All files you upload/import
- All projects you create
- Media library and assets
- AI tools and chat history
