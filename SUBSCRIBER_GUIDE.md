## Tips & Best Practices

### üéØ **Maximize Your Subscription**

1. **Use the Search Feature:** With 720 tools, searching is faster than browsing
2. **Favorite Your Tools:** Save frequently-used tools for quick access
3. **Organize Your Media Box:** Delete unused assets to free up storage
4. **Plan Before Creating:** Use story and planning tools before generating content
5. **Preview Often:** Use the preview feature to check your work as you go

### ‚ö° **Optimize Rendering**

1. **Compress Large Files:** The app auto-compresses, but smaller files render faster
2. **Use Priority Rendering:** PRO and STUDIO tiers get faster processing
3. **Export at Appropriate Quality:** Don't export 4K if you only need 1080p
4. **Trim Unused Footage:** Remove excess clips to reduce render time

### üíæ **Manage Storage**

Your subscription includes cloud storage:
- BASIC: 10GB
- PRO: 50GB
- STUDIO: 200GB

**Storage Tips:**
- Delete old projects you no longer need
- Export and download finished movies, then delete project files
- Compress images before uploading
- Use lower resolution for drafts, higher for final export
