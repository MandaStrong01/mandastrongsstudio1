## YES! You Can Upload Multiple Files

**Page 11 is your upload hub - upload as many files as you need!**

## 4 Ways to Upload on Page 11

### 1. Click "Open Files"
- Select multiple files at once
- Images, videos, audio, documents - all supported

### 2. Click "Open Photos/Videos"
- Quick access to photo library

### 3. Drag & Drop (Easiest!)
- Drag files from your computer
- Drop into Media Box area
- Upload multiple files at once

### 4. Click "Open Google Drive" (Optional)
- Requires setup (see QUICK_START_GUIDE.md)
- Import from cloud storage

## Do You Need All AI Tools?

**NO!** Only use what you need for your project.

- Making a simple video? Maybe just use AI Background Music
- Creating animation? Use Script Writer + Character Generator
- Editing existing footage? Use Video Enhancer + Color Correction

Pick and choose - don't feel pressured to use all 720 tools!
